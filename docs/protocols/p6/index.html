Additional Details

Background

Sub-seasonal prediction (week 2 - month 2) represents a challenging timescale that bridges weather forecasting and seasonal climate prediction. This “predictability gap” is increasingly important for water management and early warning systems.

Experimental Design

Protocol details are under development. The experimental design will specify:
